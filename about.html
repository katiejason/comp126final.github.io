About Us

The UNC Women's Club Lacrosse Team was founded in 2011. This team won the Division II National Championship in 2016, where we were promoted to Dividion I. Today we play against the top ranked club teams in the U.S. and are looking forward to going to many more championships.

For more information on games, to see our roster, or to learn about tryouts visit our other pages.
